## Specialty Teams

### Puppy Foster Team
*For fosters caring for puppies under 6 months*

- Email: puppyteam@pawsitivehaven.org
- Resources: Feeding schedules, socialization guides, vaccine protocols

### Senior Dog Team
*For fosters caring for dogs 8+ years*

- Email: seniors@pawsitivehaven.org
- Resources: Senior care guides, mobility support, hospice fostering

### Medical Foster Team
*For fosters caring for dogs with medical needs*

- Email: medicalfoster@pawsitivehaven.org
- Resources: Medication administration, post-surgery care, special needs

### Big Dog Team
*For fosters caring for dogs 60+ lbs*

- Email: bigdogs@pawsitivehaven.org
- Resources: Large breed handling, space requirements, exercise needs

## Communication Channels

### Slack Workspace
Join our Slack for real-time communication:
- **#general** - Organization-wide announcements
- **#foster-support** - Foster questions and support
- **#adoption-updates** - Adoption status updates
- **#vet-questions** - Quick medical questions
- **#transport-needs** - Ride requests
- **#happy-tails** - Share adoption success stories
- **#lost-found** - Missing foster dog alerts

### Facebook Groups
- **Pawsitive Haven Fosters (Private)** - Foster community
- **Pawsitive Haven Volunteers (Private)** - All volunteers
- **Pawsitive Haven Alumni (Public)** - Adopters and supporters

### Newsletter
Monthly foster newsletter sent first Monday of each month to all active fosters.
